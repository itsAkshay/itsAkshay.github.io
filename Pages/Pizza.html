PIZZA

Description

If you're looking for a homemade pizza crust recipe that's great forbeginners, you're in luck. This top-rated recipe is super easy to throw together on a whim -and it puts the store-bought stuff to shame.

INGREDIENTS

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

STEPS

  1. After mixing all the above ingredients allow your dough to rest for 30 min
  2. Prepare your surface for oil
  3. Bake!!
