Muffins

Description

Start your day in the most delicious way:With homemade banana muffins!

INGREDIENTS

  • Flour
  • Leaveners
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Egg
  • Butter

STEPS

  1. Sift the dry ingredients together in one bowl. Mix the wet ingredients in another bowl.
  2. Fold the dry mixture into the wet mixture.
  3. Spoon the batter into prepared muffin tins. Bake the muffins in the preheated oven.
  4. In an oven preheated to 350 degrees F, a dozen banana muffins should be fully baked in 25 to 30 minutes.
